The Fourth Industrial Revolution is a major historical period for modern humanity. It involves the autonomisation and increased interconnectivity of digital machines across physical, digital and biological spheres (Schwab, 2016). One of the emerging and essential disciplines during the fourth industrial revolution is robotics and artificial intelligence (AI). Robotics and AI have already been impacting various industries, such as agriculture and hospitality by improving work efficiency and allowing thousands of human jobs to vanish, bringing about a problem in socioeconomic inequality.

Robotics has an impact on the Fourth Industrial Revolution through its various applications in the agricultural industry. By 2050, the global population is expected to be 9.7 billion (United Nations, 2018). To meet this demand and serve nutritional trends, global agricultural production must increase by 70% from current levels (Ku, 2019). These statistics convey the need for farmers to develop effective and efficient farming methods to meet the future trends. Agrobot has developed the first robot to harvest strawberries, regardless of where and when they are grown. These strawberry-picking robots are able to harvest around twenty-acres of fruit in three days (Zitter, 2019), and are set for commercial use by the end of 2020 (Zitter, 2019). Moreover, some companies have developed autonomous tractors or remotely controlled tractors. The impact of the use of these technologies on the fourth industrial revolution is that greater yield is generated, compared to human workers.

The widespread application of robotics in the Fourth Industrial Revolution has a significant impact on the employees whose jobs are replaced by robots. It is estimated that robots are to replace up to 20 million factory jobs by 2030 (Cellan-Jones, 2019). This figure is concerning because it increases income inequality, and further widens the gap between the rich and the poor.

The large role which robots play in the fourth industrial revolution raises important ethical questions and ideas about the impact of robots in our future.
